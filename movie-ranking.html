The Best Movies According to Abena

My top 3 movies of all-time.

I Believe

This is my favourite movie. This is about a 9-year-old boy who experienced God's power in a supernatural way. As a result, his family and other unbelievers had an encounter with God in a wonderful way and turned to Him.

3 Idiots

Really interesting movie about 3 guys. Funny but educative.

Miss Perfect

An interesting movie about a lady who had a hard time accepting her father because he abandoned her and the mother for over 20 years. However, she faced a similar situation as her father, after breaking up with her ex and marrying a man with a delinquent daughter. Interestingly, she trained the daughter to be become a better person and they became a great family.
